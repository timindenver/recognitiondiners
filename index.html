Questions

Before you join.

01 Is there a cost to join the list?

No. Joining is free, and there's no obligation to claim a table when an invite goes out.

02 Who pays for the dinner?

Your company covers the meal as it normally would. What we handle is the reservation, the venue relationship, and the reward for booking.

03 How often do invites go out?

It varies by venue — some run every one to two weeks, others monthly. You'll only hear from us when a table's actually open.

04 What if I miss a claim window?

Tables are first come, first served, so a popular venue can fill fast. You'll stay on the list for the next drop, no penalty for missing one.
